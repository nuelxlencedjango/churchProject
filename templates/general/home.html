Love and compassion,Forgiveness and reconciliation

Jesus emphasized the significance of love, not only for God but also for each other. He instructed us to love one another, display acts of kindness and mercy, and to take care of the less fortunate. Additionally, He taught us the importance of forgiveness, encouraging us to pardon others and pursue reconciliation. He exemplified this through His own deeds, by forgiving those who persecuted Him. Mark 12:30-31,Matt 25:35-36,Matt 6:14-15

Salvation and eternal life,Humility and servanthood

Jesus proclaimed the good news of salvation, teaching that through faith in Him, man can be reconciled with God and have eternal life. He emphasized the need for repentance, turning away from sin, and placing trust in him as the path to salvation. Speaking about humility,Jesus emphasized the importance of humility and servant leadership. He demonstrated this by humbly washing the feet of His disciples and instructing them to serve others with humility and selflessness. John 3:16,Acts 3:19,Matt 20:26-28

Kingdom of God

Jesus spoke extensively about the Kingdom of God, a realm where God's sovereignty prevails and a new order is established, characterized by righteousness, peace, and justice. He instructed His disciples to prioritize the pursuit of the Kingdom of God and to align their lives with its principles. Matt 6:33.
