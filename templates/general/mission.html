Our Message

OUR MESSAGE IS ALL ABOUT JESUS CHRIST, HIS KINGDOM COMMISSION , HIS PASSION (the souls he died for ) AND HIS GLORY

  • Our Mission

    As fishers of men, our mission is to actively engage with those around us, both within our church community and in the world. We seek to be ambassadors of Christ, sharing His love, grace, and truth with others. Our purpose is to lead people to Jesus, to help them encounter His life-transforming power, and to guide them in becoming His disciples.

  • Our Purpose

    Our purpose is to bring the God-life to humanity and save them from the second death. We believe that God desires all people to experience abundant life in Him, both in this present life and for eternity. Through our words, actions, and ministry, we seek to introduce people to the transformative power of a relationship with God through Jesus Chris

    Bringing the God-life to humanity means sharing the good news of salvation and inviting others to enter into a personal relationship with God. We believe that Jesus is the way, the truth, and the life (John 14:6), and through Him, individuals can find forgiveness, reconciliation with God, and eternal life. Our purpose is to proclaim this message of hope, offering everyone the opportunity to receive God's love, grace, and redemption.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to raise an army of supernatural giants who love with passion, demonstrating the character of the Holy Spirit in this end-time move of God. We believe that we are living in a crucial time in history, where God is calling His people to rise up and be vessels of His power and glory

    Raising an army of supernatural giants means equipping and empowering believers to operate in the supernatural realm through the Holy Spirit. We believe that the same Spirit who raised Jesus from the dead dwells within us (Romans 8:11), and He desires to work through us to bring about miraculous signs, wonders, and demonstrations of His power. Our vision is to see every believer walking in their identity as sons and daughters of God, equipped with spiritual gifts, and manifesting the supernatural in their lives.

    In this end-time move of God, we envision a church that reflects the character and nature of the Holy Spirit. We desire to see believers filled with love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control (Galatians 5:22-23). Our vision is to see the fruit of the Spirit manifested in our lives and in our interactions with others, creating a culture of love, unity, and genuine relationship.

    our message is all about Jesus Christ — His person, His kingdom commission, His passion for souls, and His glory. Through our relationships, service, and commitment to the teachings of Jesus Christ, we strive to make a difference in the lives of people and bring hope to the world.
