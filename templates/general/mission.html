  • Our Vision Statement

    Raising A new generation of supernatural Giants, that are not in love with the flesh or in affinity with this present world of darkness but consumed with the love of our Heavenly Father AND THE BODY OF CHRIST.

    Raising an army of supernatural giants means equipping and empowering believers to operate in the supernatural realm through the Holy Spirit. We believe we living in the last dispensation and the end of this old world of darkness is fast approaching.

    The lord’s desires is to work through us and with us mightily bring about the supernatural like the world hasn’t seen, miraculous signs, wonders, and demonstrations of His power.

    We seek to see every believer walking in their identity as sons of God, equipped with spiritual gifts, and manifesting the supernatural in their lives growing out of babyhood.

    We reflect our kingdom culture, character and nature of the Holy Spirit. We desire to see sons of God filled with love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control (Galatians 5:22-23).
